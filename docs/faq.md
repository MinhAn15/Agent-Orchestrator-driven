# FAQ

## Who should use these templates?
Operations, support, engineering, content, and go-to-market teams that need repeatable workflows.

## Do we need a specific platform?
No. Templates are platform-agnostic and can be implemented with your current stack.

## How often should we update templates?
Review monthly, and revise whenever KPI targets are missed for two consecutive cycles.

## Can we add organization-specific steps?
Yes. Treat these templates as baselines and customize for your policies and risk profile.

## How do we track maturity?
Use KPI trends, handoff quality, SLA adherence, and reduction in manual rework.
