# Incident Response Workflow Template

## Goal
Restore service reliability rapidly while maintaining transparent communication.

## Inputs
- Monitoring alerts
- Service dependencies and runbooks
- On-call roster and escalation policy

## Workflow (Copy/Paste)
1. Detect and declare incident with initial severity.
2. Open incident channel and assign commander + scribes.
3. Identify blast radius and affected services/users.
4. Execute mitigation runbook and capture timeline events.
5. Publish status updates at fixed intervals.
6. Confirm recovery, monitor for regression, and close incident.
7. Schedule postmortem with action items and owners.

## Output
- Incident timeline
- Stakeholder communication log
- Postmortem action tracker

## KPIs
- MTTD (mean time to detect)
- MTTR (mean time to resolve)
- Postmortem action completion rate
