# Content Ops Workflow Template

## Goal
Plan, create, review, and publish content with predictable quality and timeline.

## Inputs
- Content brief
- Brand voice guide
- SEO keywords and intent
- Publishing calendar

## Workflow (Copy/Paste)
1. Parse brief and generate outline with target keyword mapping.
2. Draft content using approved tone and style checklist.
3. Run editorial QA (grammar, readability, factuality).
4. Run SEO QA (meta tags, heading hierarchy, internal links).
5. Route for human approval (editor + stakeholder).
6. Publish and log performance tracking links.

## Output
- Ready-to-publish article/post
- QA checklist report
- Performance tracking record

## KPIs
- Production cycle time
- Acceptance rate at first review
- Organic traffic growth
