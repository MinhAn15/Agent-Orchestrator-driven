# Lead Enrichment Workflow Template

## Goal
Increase lead quality by completing firmographic and intent data before sales handoff.

## Inputs
- Raw lead list (name, email, company)
- Enrichment providers/APIs
- ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) criteria

## Workflow (Copy/Paste)
1. Deduplicate leads by email/domain.
2. Enrich company data (size, industry, region, funding stage).
3. Enrich contact data (role seniority, department).
4. Score leads against ICP and intent signals.
5. Assign segment and recommended outreach playbook.
6. Push enriched leads to CRM with score + notes.

## Output
- Enriched lead records
- Lead score and segment label
- CRM sync status report

## KPIs
- Enrichment completion rate
- MQL to SQL conversion rate
- Bounce/reject rate reduction
