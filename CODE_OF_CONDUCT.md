# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment
We are committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Credit contributors fairly.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or trolling.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove or moderate content that violates this code and may restrict participation for repeated violations.
